✨ Concept 3: Exposure Adjustment Animation + Hand Selection

Complete Animation Sequence:

1. FLANEUR 718 title corrects from overexposed to proper exposure (1s) - starts bright/blown-out, fades to solid black with slight blur-to-sharp effect

2. Film Strip 1 appears with photo flash effect (1s delay, 0.4s duration)

3. Film Strip 2 appears with photo flash effect (1.25s delay, 0.4s duration)

4. Film Strip 3 appears with photo flash effect (1.5s delay, 0.4s duration)

5. Tagline fades up from bottom (1.9s delay, 0.6s duration)

6. Red grease pencil marks draw on one by one, simulating hand-selection (starts at 2.6s):

• Frame 15A marked (2.6s, 0.6s duration)
• Frame 8A marked (3.0s, 0.6s duration)
• Frame 12 marked (3.4s, 0.6s duration)
• Frame 5 marked (3.8s, 0.6s duration)

Total Duration: ~4.4 seconds (main sequence 2.5s + hand selection 1.9s)

Why this works: The exposure correction and sequential photo flashes establish photographic expertise, then the hand-drawn selection marks complete the authentic darkroom workflow - as if you're watching a photographer review their contact sheet and mark keepers. The organic, hand-drawn animation reinforces the craft and intentionality of film photography.
